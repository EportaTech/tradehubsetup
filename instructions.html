Tradehub Site Integration

There are two steps to integrating Tradehub into your site, adding the tracking pixel followed by adding links where you would like to direct customers to the trade site.

Tracking pixel

To add the tracking pixel, the following code should be added across all pages. You can place the script alongside any other analytics/tracking code you use, or just after the opening <body> tag.

<script async defer crossorigin="anonymous" src="https://{{you-company-name}}.eporta.com/buttons/buttonScriptV2.js"></script>

Adding links

General catalogue link

For a general catalogue link, you simply link to the URL https://{{you-company-name}}.eporta.com/buttons/inbound. The customer will then be redirected to the catalogue page.

Product specific link

To link to a specific product page within your Tradehub, you will need your SKU or product ID (these are the ones sent to us in your data upload).

The URL for these links includes the product-id query string parameter, which should be replace by the relevant SKU/ID for the product you want to link to.

The link should be of the form https://{{you-company-name}}.eporta.com/buttons/inbound?product-id=your-product-id.
